Which is the best account type?

Alphforex provides different accounts with different spreads and conditions so you can choose the best one based on your needs and trading goals.

With our Standard, Premium, Privilege and Cent accounts you can access CFDs on forex, metals, indices, commodities, futures and shares. The type of account that is right for you also depends on your risk tolerance, your initial investment size and the available time you have to trade on a daily basis.

If you’ve just started trading in the forex markets, you’ll need to choose an account type that is best suited to your skill, knowledge and experience. Our accounts have zero commissions and instant execution.
Trading lots start from 0.01 for all account types.

Live and Cent Accounts

Our Live accounts can be separated into fixed and floating depending on the spread. Our Cent account is similar to our Live accounts, with the only difference that dollars are denominated in cents. For example, if you deposit $100, this will be converted and added to the Cent account as 10,000 cents. Trading on the Cent account is a great way to start trading if you are a beginner.
